DESCRIPTION

We make eye opening games.

Kaigan Games is one of the leading indie game developers in South East Asia. Over the past 6 years, our games like Sara is Missing and SIMULACRA, has garnered over 7 international awards, millions of downloads with and a growing Discord fanbase with more than 10,000 members. Besides making our own games, our specialization in the “found phone” genre has led us to work with clients around the world such as UNHCR, Samsung and the BBC.

The secret to our success lies with the strength of our employees, therefore their goals and wellbeing remains our priority. We strive to create a promising environment for growth, enhancement while maintaining a healthy work-life balance.

If you’re looking for a rewarding career path in the games industry, we can assure you that this is it. Join us to experience a rewarding career path!
